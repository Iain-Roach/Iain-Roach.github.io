Overview

Ninjas On Trampolines is a party platform fighter built in Unity. It uses simple controls with fast paced combat to take on the role of a ninja, wielding a paintbrush. Your goal is to cover the background with as much of your paint as possible by defeating your friends.

Goals

  • Create a game that could be fun no matter what the player skill was.
  • Learn about developing multiplayer games.
  • Publish the finished product.

Detailed Info

Ninjas on Trampolines started out as a class project but has evolved into a game that is planned to be released summer 2024. The game got into the MAGIC maker program at the Rochester Institute of Technology where it further evolved to where it is today.

Design

Gamemodes

The Free For All or Core gamemode of Ninjas on Trampolines is very simple, you attack another player and get your paint color on the background. With whoever has the most of their color on the environment by the end of the match is the victor.

In designing the gamemodes I wanted to build onto that core gameplay loop where the player can interact with the gamemode in a simple way and the overarching goal of the gamemode will be the same as the core gamemode.The Spray-king gamemode is just the Free For All gamemode of Ninjas on Trampolines but there is a new permanent item in the match. The spraycan. Players can pick up the spraycan and it allows them to passively paint the environment as they run around. If another player kills the player holding the spraycan will be able to pick up the spraycan.

For the paintball gamemode I wanted to add a more chaotic element into the game that is still interacted with in a simple manner. The Paintball bounces around the stage and can be hit by a player to send it flying, bouncing around the stage, and turn it into their color. While the paintball is in this state it will passively paint onto the environemnt as well as kill anyplayer that comes into contact that doesn't share a color.

Items

Items can randomly spawn around each level, with their currently being three different items, a paintbomb, a sprinkler, and a mop. Players can pick up the items after they spawn and use the attack button to use the item. If a player is killed when they are holding an item the item will drop for other players to pickup. If the item is a throwable item it can be thrown in any of the four cardinal directions.

The Paintbomb is a throwable item that when it makes contact with another player or wall will explode, both killing players and painting the environment within a certain radius. The radius of the paintbomb increases depending on how many players are in its blast radius, incentivising players to go for multi-kill throws. The paintbomb can also bounce off of the trampolines which can lead to some cool kills.

The Sprinkler is another throwable item that sticks into walls and will spray out paint at intervals in a 180 degree arc away from the wall the sprinkler is stuck in. The sprinkler can also bounce off of trampolines.

The mop is a single use item that gets used as soon as the player presses the attack button, the mop will remove all paint around the player indiscriminently.

Future Additions

  • Allow players to "parry" items.
  • Allow players to interact with the sprinkler after it has been thrown.
  • Add items to networking.
