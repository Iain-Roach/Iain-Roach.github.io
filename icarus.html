Overview

Icarus is a single player archery game built in Unity for the Meta Quest 2. It attempts to mimic real life archery mechanics as you draw arrows from a quiver on your back and notch them into the bow. Your goal is to hit targets as quickly and accurately as possible to compete for the top spot on the leaderboards.

Goals

  • Create a finished game built for a virtual reality platform.
  • Create intuitive mechanics based on real life methods.
  • Create a fun virtual reality game that attempts to reduce motion sickness as much as possible.
